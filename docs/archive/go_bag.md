# Go-Bag Checklist — Garden Grove MMA Tank Incident

**Why this exists:** You're outside the evac zone but downwind. The zone has already expanded once. If it expands to your address, you may have minutes, not hours, to leave. This is the bag you grab and walk out with. Pre-pack it now; check it once a day while the incident is active.

**Print this page** and tape it to the inside of your front door. The dashboard is for when you have time to think; this is for when you don't.

---

## Documents (in a sealed plastic bag, in the bag)

- [ ] Driver's license / passport
- [ ] Insurance card (health, auto, renter's/homeowner's)
- [ ] Phone with charging cable + power bank (charged)
- [ ] Cash ($100+ in small bills — ATMs may be down)
- [ ] Backup credit/debit card
- [ ] Printed copy of: emergency contact phone numbers, your doctor, your pharmacy
- [ ] A USB stick or written copy of your last 3 important emails / docs (insurance correspondence, lease, anything you'd hate to lose)

## Medications + medical

- [ ] **All prescription medications you take daily** — minimum 5-day supply
- [ ] Reading glasses / spare contacts + solution
- [ ] First aid kit (small)
- [ ] N95 or KN95 masks (3–5) — important given chemical inhalation risk
- [ ] Hand sanitizer

## Clothes (1 day's worth)

- [ ] Sturdy shoes you can walk distance in
- [ ] Layers — long sleeves + long pants (covers skin if exposed to MMA vapor)
- [ ] Underwear, socks
- [ ] Light jacket
- [ ] Hat

## Water + food

- [ ] 1–2 bottles of water minimum
- [ ] 3–4 protein bars / shelf-stable snacks
- [ ] Pet food if applicable (and pet meds!)

## Communication + comfort

- [ ] Phone charger (USB-C / Lightning — both if you have multiple devices)
- [ ] Battery pack
- [ ] Small flashlight + spare batteries
- [ ] Earplugs (shelters are loud)
- [ ] Eye mask
- [ ] Book / kindle / something to settle your nervous system

## Pets

- [ ] Carrier or leash
- [ ] Food + bowl
- [ ] Vaccination records (some shelters require)
- [ ] Comfort toy / blanket

## Keys + access

- [ ] Spare house key (in case you need to come back through a checkpoint)
- [ ] Car keys (in the bag, not on the hook by the door — grab and go)
- [ ] Key to a friend/family member's house you'd shelter with

## In the moment

If you get an evacuation notification:

1. Grab the go-bag (and your pets).
2. Close all windows; turn off HVAC.
3. Take the keys. Lock the door.
4. **Drive AWAY FROM the tank.** Tank is at GKN Aerospace, Garden Grove — generally in the area bounded by Trask / Ball / Valley View / Dale. Pick a route that increases distance.
5. Avoid the freeways immediately east/west of the tank — they will be jammed with everyone else evacuating. Surface streets going perpendicular to the obvious escape direction are often faster.
6. Once safe, text someone "I'm out, headed to [destination]." Then call.

## After

- [ ] Save the go-bag contents — don't disperse them back to the house — until the incident is officially over.
- [ ] If you smelled anything chemical or felt any symptoms during evacuation, see a doctor and DOCUMENT it (date, time, what you smelled, where you were). MMA exposure documentation could matter for insurance or legal purposes later.

**Update this checklist** based on what you actually needed if you do end up evacuating. Then archive it. Next emergency, you'll start from a better baseline.
