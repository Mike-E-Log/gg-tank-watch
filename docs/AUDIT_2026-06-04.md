Exhaustive all-device UI/UX sweep + comprehensive info/links audit of the frozen
archive at https://gg-tank-watch.vercel.app. Method: Playwright `channel:'msedge'`
geometry sweep (108 combos), parallel curl link-liveness (112 URLs), a 3-agent
research Workflow (provenance / facts / contacts), a rendered-DOM accessibility

**light + dark** × **9 surfaces** (Map; News ×4 filters All/Official/Articles/Videos;
Info ×4 sub-tabs Summary/Officials/Resources/About, scroll-complete) = **108 renders**.

  light / dark: official-first ordering, OFFICIAL badges, date-only, contrast, tap targets,
  MapLibre render (OSM tiles + evac box + shelter markers), recovery-first Resources, "(May 2026)" anchors.
- **Intentional, left as-is:** (a) at 320px the masthead dateline "Historical archive · May 21–26, 2026"
  truncates the year with an ellipsis (`nowrap/overflow:hidden/text-overflow:ellipsis`); the year is
  redundant with the ARCHIVE pill + "Resolved May 26, 2026" below. (b) Map legend "Former evac area /
  Former shelter" vs Resources "(May 2026)" wording mismatch — a deliberate context-driven choice.

No genuinely-new UI issues → no UI fixes required (frozen surface).

## 3. Provenance — ONE CRITICAL fabrication (the headline finding)

The project's core safety claim is "no fabricated sources; every provenance claim is true."
**One item refutes it.** Found in the audit and independently confirmed by the provenance agent.

- **`data/news_archive.json` line ~1503 — "School Closure Notice — Orange County Department of Education".**
  URL: `…/several-garden-grove-unified-school-campuses-closed-following-chemical-leak-response/` → **HTTP 404.**
  Yet its `provenance.url_status` = `"agent-verified-resolves"` with a detailed `url_status_note` asserting
  WebFetch-confirmed specifics (12 GGUSD campuses closed May 22; 9 reopened May 26; 3 closed through May 27).
  **A 404 page cannot have produced those details → the provenance record is fabricated.** SEVERITY: CRITICAL.
  - Root cause: a near-duplicate slug. The live OCDE URL `…/several-garden-grove-unified-campuses-closed-…/`
    (no "school-") returns 200 but its current content is the **May-26 evac-lifted update**, already used by a
    different item (line ~208, correctly titled and verified). A one-word transcription error cascaded into a
    false verification claim.
- Otherwise provenance is sound: 92 unique URLs, no duplicates, all items have URLs, and "unchecked" items
  correctly avoid asserting specific verified detail (fabrication is isolated to this one item).

## 6. Static-page drift — `terms.html` & `accessibility.html` over-describe the app

Both pages say "Last updated: 2026-05-26" but describe a **pre-conduit-pivot, more-featured app**. The main
`dashboard.html` is impeccably honest; these two satellite pages drifted because no eval guard checks their content.

**terms.html** references removed features:
- §2 + banner: the **"check your address" tool** and **"blast and plume estimates"** (lines 71, 93–94).
  These were removed in the conduit pivot — confirmed: 0 such code in dashboard.html. SEVERITY: MAJOR (a legal
  page describing capabilities the app does not have, on an honesty-thesis project).

**accessibility.html** makes false/stale claims:
- "**Vietnamese language support for the full interface**" — FALSE; the app is **English-only by design** (G1),
  enforced by `eval/test_no_vietnamese_residue.py`. SEVERITY: CRITICAL (contradicts the G1 safety constraint).
- "Dark mode and light mode, **respecting system preference**" — FALSE for the dashboard (manual toggle, default
  light, no `prefers-color-scheme`). SEVERITY: MINOR.
- "interactive **Leaflet** map" — WRONG; it is **MapLibre GL** (0 Leaflet refs). SEVERITY: MINOR.
- map text-alternative describes "**wind conditions**" — STALE; the wind indicator was removed
  (`eval/test_wind_removed.py`). SEVERITY: MINOR.

> Note: the satellite pages also theme via `prefers-color-scheme` while the dashboard uses a manual toggle — a
> cross-page theming inconsistency (minor).

## 7. Accessibility — strong (dashboard)

Rendered-DOM probe (375px): `lang="en"`; single `<h1>`; no skipped heading levels; **all 14 images have alt**;
**every visible button/link has an accessible name**; 2 tablists with `role=tab`/`aria-selected`/`aria-controls`;
map canvas `aria-label="Map"`; `prefers-reduced-motion` honored in CSS; 2 `aria-live` regions; skip-to-content link.
- Minor: header share/theme buttons render 36×30px — above the WCAG 2.1 AA target (24×24) but below AAA (44×44).
- The Info tab carries the shelter/zone text the map cannot convey by keyboard (a real text alternative path).

## 8. Security posture — positive (record in README)

Strong CSP on prod: `default-src 'self'`, `X-Frame-Options: DENY` (so the page cannot be framed — which is also
why iframe-based mobile QA fails and the Playwright-viewport path is required). No third-party trackers; no ads;
no PII collection (per terms §8). These are genuine safety positives worth naming in the README.
