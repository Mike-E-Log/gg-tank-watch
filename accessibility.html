Accessibility

GG Tank Watch — an independent, volunteer-built community resource.

Our commitment

GG Tank Watch is built for the whole community, including people with disabilities. During an emergency, accessible information is not optional. We aim to conform to WCAG 2.1 Level AA.

What we do

  • Semantic HTML with proper heading hierarchy and landmark regions.
  • Keyboard-navigable tabs (Map, News, Info) with ARIA roles and states.
  • A skip-to-content link for keyboard users.
  • Live regions (aria-live) that announce banner updates and status changes to screen readers.
  • Text contrast targeting WCAG AA (4.5:1 minimum for normal text).
  • Decorative SVGs marked aria-hidden="true".
  • Dark mode and light mode, respecting system preference.
  • Vietnamese language support for the full interface.
  • A text alternative for the interactive map describing the evacuation zone, shelter locations, and wind conditions.

Known limitations

  • The interactive Leaflet map is not fully operable by keyboard alone. Shelter addresses and evacuation zone details are available in text form on the Info tab.
  • Some third-party embedded content (news videos, social media posts) may not meet accessibility standards.

Feedback

If you encounter an accessibility barrier on this site, please email ggtankwatch@gmail.com. We take accessibility reports seriously and will work to address them promptly.
